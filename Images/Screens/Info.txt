How to make outline in GIMP
Rightclick on wanted texts layer -> Alfa to Marking -> rightclick on the marking in picture/workspace -> Marking -> Enlarge -> 2px, OK
Make a new layer and fill with bucket for the wanted colour, order the new layer under the text layer in list
